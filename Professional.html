Mission

My mission, should I choose to accept it is, to bring about widespread technological development and adoption in Jamaica, with the goal
of transforming the country into a technologically advanced society that is on par with first-world countries. To this I am:

  • Focused on transforming the education sector to keep generalized knowledge at the primary and secondary level
  • Focused on implementing a HEART like structure at the tertiary that teaches you what you need to know for a specific career,
    nothing more, nothing less
  • Going to implement higher internet speeds, modern communication networks and reliable electricity to facilitate the support of
    technological development and adoption
  • Encouraging the development of a strong entrepreneurial ecosystem in Jamaica, where entrepreneurs can access funding, mentorship,
    and support to bring their innovative ideas to life.
  • Fostering a culture of innovation by supporting research and development initiatives in areas such as artificial intelligence,
    biotechnology, and renewable energy. This can help to attract investment, create jobs, and drive economic growth.
